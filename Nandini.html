INTRODUCTION:-

Web development refers to building, creating, and an maintaining websites. It includes aspects such as web design, web publishing, web programming, and database management. While the terms "web developer" and "web designer" are often used synonymously, they do not mean the same thing.

IS WEB DEVELOPMENT A GOOD CARRER

Currently, skilled web-developers enjoy great demand in the job market, and you can easily make a six-figure income by working as a web developer. It is safe to say that web development is an ideal choice for individuals who have just stepped into the IT industry.
